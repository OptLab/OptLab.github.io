The structure of the MIM waveguide with a channel drop filter

  • The incident wave is coupled into the waveguide, and the SPP wave forms on the metal interfaces .
  • The incident power is partly coupled into the nanocavity nearby the bus waveguide.
  • The nanocavity functions as a resonator and can transmit the special light signals to the drop waveguide.
